Nice to meet you!

Hi we are Rai, Zahra, Gan and Joanne, the co-founders of Filmists. We created this platform because we realized something was missing: an online space where film lovers like you could connect, share your thoughts and build a community.

Our goal

At Filmists, our primary goal is to be the ultimate hub for movie lovers. We strive to create a platform where you can effortlessly:

Discover the best movies
Discuss your favorite films
Connect with others

We want to build a vibrant community where movie lovers can exchange thoughts and recommendations, truly connecting over their shared appreciation for film. Our aim is to make exploring and discussing movies an engaging, shared experience for everyone.

Filmists community

Whether you're a casual viewer, a devoted cinephile, or someone just beginning to explore the magic of film, Filmists welcomes you. We believe that every opinion matters whether you're reacting to the latest big release or uncovering a hidden cinematic treasure.


Filmists is built to be more than a platform, it’s a space where real conversations about film can thrive. We aim to bring together unique perspectives and make people feel heard. Here you’re not just a user: you’re a part of a growing community.
